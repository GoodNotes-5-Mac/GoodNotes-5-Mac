## 📖 Overview

<a href="#goodNotes5">GoodNotes 5</a> serves students, professionals, academics, and creatives who want to combine the tactile experience of handwriting with the organizational advantages of digital storage — primarily iPad users who write with Apple Pencil but who access, organize, and manage their notes from Mac as well.

The <a href="#goodNotes5">student note-taking use case</a> is GoodNotes's largest market — students who take lecture notes by hand on iPad with Apple Pencil and study from those notes, annotate PDF textbooks and lecture slides, and organize their academic notes by subject and semester. The searchable handwriting makes studying from GoodNotes notes more efficient than physical notebooks where finding specific content requires flipping through pages. The <a href="#goodNotes5">professional meeting notes use case</a> applies GoodNotes to meeting annotation — annotating agenda PDFs, capturing handwritten notes in a styled notebook, and maintaining organized meeting records by project and date that are searchable and accessible from Mac between meetings.

The <a href="#goodNotes5">academic and researcher workflow</a> uses GoodNotes for PDF research paper annotation — marking up papers with handwritten notes, highlighting key passages, and maintaining an annotated research library organized by topic. The annotation appears in the PDF export, making GoodNotes-annotated papers shareable with collaborators. <a href="#goodNotes5">Creative journaling and personal use</a> draws on GoodNotes's custom templates, beautiful notebook covers, and the genuine pleasure of handwriting in a well-designed digital environment for personal journals, sketchbooks, and creative note-taking beyond professional contexts.</p>

<a href="#goodNotes5">Page templates and covers marketplace</a> provides a community ecosystem of custom templates and notebook cover designs — specialty templates for bullet journaling, fitness tracking, recipe organization, study planning, and many other personal and professional note-taking frameworks. <a href="#goodNotes5">Export options</a> share notes as PDF, images, or GoodNotes files — exporting annotated PDFs with embedded handwriting, sharing pages as images, or transferring complete notebooks to collaborators and between devices.

<a href="#goodNotes5">Zoom writing mode</a> magnifies a portion of the page for writing in small spaces — enabling precise, detailed handwriting in constrained areas without moving the page view that would disturb the overall layout. <a href="#goodNotes5">One-time purchase model</a> provides permanent GoodNotes 5 access — a single purchase covers ongoing use on Mac and iPad with updates included, without subscription costs.</p>

<summary>How does GoodNotes 5 compare to Notability?</summary>

Both are leading iPad and Mac note-taking apps with handwriting and PDF annotation. GoodNotes has stronger notebook organization and template ecosystem; Notability has audio recording integration. Both are excellent — preference depends on specific workflow needs.

</details>

Yes. Presentation mode displays the current page full-screen for presenting notes, PDFs, or sketches.
